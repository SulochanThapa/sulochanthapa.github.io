About Orchid Ghar & Our Founder

Founder & Orchid Expert

Orchid Ghar is a premium florist specializing in exotic orchids and fresh flowers, delivering across India. Based in Rongmook Ceder T.E, Sonada, Darjeeling, we take pride in offering a wide variety of handpicked orchids, bringing nature’s elegance to your home.

Founded by Pranay Rai, our mission is to make high-quality orchids & other flowers accessible to flower lovers, event planners, and nature enthusiasts across India. Whether you need stunning orchids for home décor, a thoughtful gift, or bulk flowers for events and weddings, we ensure the finest blooms.

We specialize in organic Cymbidium orchids, Lalupatey, and seasonal flowers, all grown sustainably and delivered fresh. Serving regions including Darjeeling, Sikkim, Kurseong, Sonada, Kalimpong, Assam, Shillong, Meghalaya, and Arunachal Pradesh, we bring nature’s finest blooms straight to your doorstep.
