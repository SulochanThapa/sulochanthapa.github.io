🌟 Indian Orchid Festival Sikkim 2025 – King of the Show

The first-ever Indian Orchid Festival 2025 was organized by the ICAR-National Research Centre for Orchids in Pakyong, Sikkim, from March 1–4, 2025. The festival featured an orchid show-cum-competition with 102 participants across 19 categories.

Source: Indian Council of Agricultural Research
