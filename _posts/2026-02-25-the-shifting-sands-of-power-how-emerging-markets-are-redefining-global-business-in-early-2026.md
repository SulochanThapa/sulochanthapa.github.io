<p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">This shift isn't accidental. These countries are increasingly forging South-South cooperation, creating new trade routes, and developing financial mechanisms that reduce reliance on established global currencies and financial systems. This focus on self-reliance and diversified partnerships forms the bedrock of their growing economic clout.</p>
