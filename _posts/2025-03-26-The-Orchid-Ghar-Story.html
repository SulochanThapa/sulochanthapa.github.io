The Orchid Ghar Story

Cultivating Organic Beauty in the Heart of Darjeeling

Nestled amidst the misty hills of Darjeeling

Orchid Ghar is not just a florist—it’s a living testament to nature’s elegance and sustainable growth. Founded by orchid expert Pranay Rai in the enchanting locale of Rongmook Ceder Tea Estate, Sonada, Darjeeling, Orchid Ghar has quickly become synonymous with organic Cymbidium orchids, Lalupatey, and a curated selection of seasonal flowers.

A Blooming Passion for Organic Orchids

Growing up surrounded by the lush landscapes of Darjeeling, Pranay Rai developed an innate fascination for the region’s natural flora. Among all the botanical wonders, the delicate Cymbidium orchid caught his eye. Pranay’s vision was clear from the start: to offer only the finest organic orchids grown without the use of harmful chemicals, ensuring both beauty and environmental responsibility.

Embracing Sustainability: The Orchid Ghar Ethos

At Orchid Ghar, every plant is nurtured with an unwavering commitment to organic and sustainable practices. The team uses natural fertilizers and eco-friendly cultivation methods to bring out the best in every Cymbidium orchid, Lalupatey, and seasonal flower.

Organic Growth in the Himalayan Foothills

The cool, misty climate of Darjeeling creates an ideal environment for growing orchids organically. The expansive poly-houses at Orchid Ghar are designed to mimic the natural habitat of these exquisite plants, ensuring optimal growth through sustainable practices.

More Than Just Orchids: A Symphony of Seasonal Flowers

While organic Cymbidium orchids are the star of the show, Orchid Ghar’s offerings extend beyond. They also specialize in Lalupatey—a rare and charming flower—along with a rotating selection of seasonal blooms that reflect nature’s changing rhythm.

Handpicked, Fresh, and Delivered with Care

Every bouquet, arrangement, and floral gift is curated with precision, ensuring only the freshest blooms reach customers. Orchid Ghar’s commitment to quality has earned them a loyal customer base across India.

Celebrating the Orchid Legacy at the Indian Orchid Festival

In early 2025, Orchid Ghar was featured at the Indian Orchid Festival in Sikkim, organized by the ICAR-National Research Centre for Orchids. Pranay Rai’s dedication to sustainable orchid cultivation earned him national recognition in the world of floriculture.

Bringing Nature Home: The Future of Orchid Ghar

Orchid Ghar’s mission is to deepen the connection between people and nature through organic floriculture. With plans to expand and innovate, they continue to prove that sustainability and beauty can go hand in hand.
