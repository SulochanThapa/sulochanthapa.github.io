Best Orchids in Darjeeling & Sikkim

Explore the rarest & most exotic orchids in the Eastern Himalayas!

Cymbidium Orchid (The Jewel Orchid)

Found in higher altitudes of Sikkim & Darjeeling. Blooms in winter, perfect for festivals & decorations.

Dendrobium Orchid (Everlasting Beauty)

Grows in cool & warm climates. Stunning purple, pink, and white blooms.

Vanda Orchid (Air Orchid)

Thrives without soil, absorbing nutrients from air & water! Famous for vibrant blue & violet flowers.

Paphiopedilum (Lady’s Slipper Orchid)

One of the rarest orchids in India. Found in moist, shaded forests.
