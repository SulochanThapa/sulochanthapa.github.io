<ul class="list-disc list-inside mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">

<li><strong>Diversify Your Watchlist:</strong> Don't stick to just one genre. Explore a mix of dramas, comedies, documentaries, and international films to broaden your horizons and prevent viewer fatigue.</li>
