Cortina d'Ampezzo, a legendary resort, notably hosted the Winter Games back in 1956, making this a significant anniversary and a blending of tradition with a forward-looking vision. The selection of these diverse locations promises not only stunning backdrops for competition but also a unique opportunity to showcase Italy's breathtaking natural beauty and vibrant urban life to the world.</p>
<p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">The organizers have emphasized a commitment to sustainability, leveraging existing infrastructure where possible and focusing on a minimal environmental footprint. <p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">The men's and women's ice hockey tournaments are consistently among the most highly anticipated events, known for their fierce rivalries and dramatic finishes. <p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">Combining athleticism, artistry, and emotional storytelling, figure skating remains a perennial favorite. The individual and pairs competitions, as well as ice dance, will mesmerize audiences with their intricate routines and breathtaking performances.</p>

<p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">These endurance events, set against stunning snowy landscapes, test athletes' stamina and shooting accuracy (in biathlon). They offer a profound appreciation for the raw power and mental fortitude required in winter sports.</p>
