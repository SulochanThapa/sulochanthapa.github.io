Top Orchid Care Tips – Do’s and Don’ts

Unlock the secrets to growing vibrant and healthy orchids!

✅ DO’S:

  • ✔ Place orchids in bright, indirect sunlight.
  • ✔ Use drainage holes in pots to prevent root rot.
  • ✔ Maintain warm temperatures (15°C–30°C).
  • ✔ Mist orchids lightly to boost humidity.

❌ DON’TS:

  • ❌ Never overwater—roots should never sit in water!
  • ❌ Avoid keeping orchids near heaters or AC vents.
  • ❌ Don’t use regular garden soil—orchids need air circulation.
  • ❌ Never cut a healthy green stem—trim only dead parts.
🌱 Pro Tip: Rotate your orchid pot every few weeks for even light exposure & balanced growth!

Orchids are resilient yet delicate—give them love, and they’ll reward you with breathtaking blooms! 🌸
