<p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">Given its location in the Amazon, COP30 is placing a strong emphasis on nature-based solutions. Indigenous communities, often stewards of these vital ecosystems, are playing a more prominent role in advocating for their rights and knowledge in climate solutions.</p>
